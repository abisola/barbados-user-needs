Register for the Youth MC Training Programme

Last updated: 12 May 2026

Overview

The Youth MC Training Programme trains young Barbadians to MC, present and host – on stage, on radio, and online. It is run by the Ministry of Youth, Sports and Community Empowerment.

Training covers the basics of stage presence, scriptwriting, voice control, audience interaction and working with a production team. Participants get coaching from established MCs and presenters.

The 2025 form is currently not open. Check back when registration opens.

The programme is open to people aged 14 to 25. If you are under 16, a parent or guardian needs to register you and give their consent.

Who can apply

  • Barbadian nationals aged 14 to 25
  • parents or guardians registering a young person under 16

Before you start

You will need to know:

  • the participant’s full name, address and date of birth
  • their National Registration Number
  • their phone number and email
  • their level of experience with public speaking or hosting
  • for under-16s, the parent or guardian’s details

Documents you need

Bring with you (or upload):

  • Photo ID
  • Birth certificate For participants under 18.
  • Parental consent form For participants under 16.
  • Short voice or video clip Optional – introducing yourself.

Cost

There is no fee.

How long it takes

Places are usually confirmed within 2 weeks of the closing date.

How to apply

Online when registration opens

Complete the Youth MC Training Programme Registration Form on the Ministry website during the registration window.
