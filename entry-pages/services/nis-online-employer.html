Register for Online Social Security (employer)

Last updated: 12 May 2026

Overview

The NIS Online Social Security Service has a separate area for employers. With an account, you can submit your monthly contribution returns, pay contributions and register new hires without coming into the office.

You need to register your business once. The authorised contact named on the form will be the person who logs in and runs the account.

Bring your certificate of incorporation or business registration, plus photo ID for the authorised contact. If the contact changes later, send a new registration form to update the record.

Most active employers in Barbados now manage their NIS obligations through this portal.

Before you start

You will need to know:

  • your business name
  • your employer’s NIS number
  • your business registration number
  • the address and parish of the business
  • an email address and phone number for an authorised contact

Documents you need

Bring with you:

  • Certificate of incorporation or business registration
  • Photo ID for the authorised contact

Cost

There is no fee.

How long it takes

Your account is usually active within 5 working days.

How to apply

In person

Take the completed Online Social Security Service Registration (Employer) form to the National Insurance Office.
