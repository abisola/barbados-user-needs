Transfer a vehicle (no money exchanged)

Last updated: 12 May 2026

Overview

If you are giving a vehicle to someone with no money changing hands – for example, to a family member or as a gift – you still need to tell the Barbados Licensing Authority.

The Transfer of Vehicle form is similar to the Sale of Vehicle form, but it is used only when there is no payment. Like the sale form, there is a single-owner version and a joint-owner version.

After the transfer, the new owner has to register the vehicle in their own name. They will not be able to do that until your transfer has been recorded.

Section 18 of the Road Traffic Act requires this notification. The Licensing Authority will record the transfer and update the registration of the vehicle.

Before you start

You will need to know:

  • your full name, address, ID number, telephone number and email
  • if there is more than one owner, the details of every owner
  • the registration number, engine number and chassis number
  • the full name and address of the person taking the vehicle
  • whether you want to keep your registration number

Documents you need

Bring with you:

  • Registration card or disc
  • Photo ID for each owner

Cost

There is no fee.

How long it takes

The transfer is recorded the same day.

How to apply

In person

Take the completed Transfer of Vehicle form (single owner or joint owner version) to the Barbados Licensing Authority.
