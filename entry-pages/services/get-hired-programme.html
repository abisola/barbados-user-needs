Apply for the Get Hired Programme

Last updated: 12 May 2026

Overview

Get Hired is a Ministry of Youth, Sports and Community Empowerment programme that helps young Barbadians prepare for and find work. It runs in cohorts each year.

Participants get training in workplace skills – things like CV writing, interview practice, professional communication and basic digital skills – and a placement with an employer to put the training into practice.

The programme is open to Barbadians who are out of work, looking to change careers, or new to the job market. There is no fee, and you do not need formal qualifications to apply.

Applications open at set times each year and run for several weeks. You will hear back from the Ministry within four to six weeks of the closing date.

Who can apply

  • Barbadian nationals aged 18 to 35
  • people who are out of work or looking to change careers

Before you start

You will need to know:

  • your full name, address and date of birth
  • your National Registration Number
  • your email address and phone number
  • your highest level of education and any qualifications
  • your work history, including dates and roles
  • the area of work you want to go into

Documents you need

Bring with you (or upload):

  • National ID card
  • Birth certificate
  • Most recent CV
  • Certificates for your qualifications
  • Recent passport-size photograph

Cost

There is no fee.

How long it takes

Applications are usually open for several weeks. You will get a reply within 4 to 6 weeks of the closing date.

How to apply

Online

Complete the Get Hired Programme: Application Form (2026) on the Ministry website.
