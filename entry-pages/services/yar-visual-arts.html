Register for Youth Achieving Results (Visual Arts)

Last updated: 12 May 2026

Overview

Youth Achieving Results (YAR) supports young Barbadians in the arts. The Visual Arts strand is for people working in painting, drawing, photography, sculpture and digital art.

You get classes with experienced practitioners, mentoring from established artists, and a chance to show your work at end-of-programme exhibitions.

The programme is open to people aged 12 to 30. If you are under 16, a parent or guardian needs to register you.

There is no fee. You can include a portfolio or sample of recent work with your application if you have one – it helps the team understand where you are and how the programme can support you.

Who can apply

  • Barbadian nationals aged 12 to 30
  • parents or guardians registering a child under 16

Before you start

You will need to know:

  • the participant’s full name, address and date of birth
  • their National Registration Number
  • their phone number and email
  • the visual art they want to focus on (painting, drawing, photography, sculpture, digital art)
  • their level of experience
  • for under-16s, the parent or guardian’s details

Documents you need

Bring with you (or upload):

  • Photo ID
  • Birth certificate For participants under 18.
  • Parental consent form For participants under 16.
  • Portfolio or sample of recent work Optional but helpful.

Cost

There is no fee.

How long it takes

Places are usually confirmed within 2 weeks of the closing date.

How to apply

Online

Complete the Youth Achieving Results (Visual Arts): Registration Form on the Ministry website.
