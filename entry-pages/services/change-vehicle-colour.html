Change the colour of your vehicle

Last updated: 12 May 2026

Overview

Your registration record includes the colour of your vehicle. If you respray it or wrap it in a different colour, you must tell the Barbados Licensing Authority so the record matches the vehicle.

This is a short form. You confirm who you are, the registration number, the current colour and the new colour. The Licensing Authority updates the record and approves the change.

It matters because traffic enforcement and the police rely on the registration record. If your vehicle is stopped or stolen, the colour on file needs to match what the vehicle actually looks like now.

Before you start

You will need to know:

  • your full name and address
  • the registration number of the vehicle
  • the current colour and the new colour
  • the date you plan to make the change

Documents you need

Bring with you:

  • Photo ID
  • Registration card or disc

Cost

There is no fee.

How long it takes

Approval is given the same day.

How to apply

In person

Take the completed Request to Change the Colour of Vehicle form to the Barbados Licensing Authority.
