Submit a BYAC reference form

Last updated: 12 May 2026

Overview

Every BYAC applicant needs two referees. The referees are people who know the applicant well and can speak honestly about their character, attitude and how they are likely to do in the programme.

Good referees include teachers, employers, pastors, coaches and youth leaders. The form asks them to say how long they have known the applicant and in what capacity.

Referees should not be relatives. The Ministry wants an independent view, not a family endorsement.

The referee can send the completed form straight to BYAC, or give it back to the applicant in a sealed envelope to submit with their other documents.

Who can apply

  • people who have known the applicant for at least 2 years
  • you should not be a relative of the applicant

Before you start

You will need to know:

  • the applicant’s full name
  • how long you have known the applicant
  • in what capacity you have known them (for example, teacher, employer, pastor)
  • your honest view of their character, attitude and ability
  • your full name, address, phone number and email

Documents you need

You will need:

  • Your own photo ID
  • Letterhead If you are giving the reference in your professional capacity.

Cost

There is no fee.

How long it takes

Fill in the form as soon as possible so the applicant’s application is not held up.

How to apply

Return the completed form to BYAC

Send the completed Reference Form to BYAC by post or email, or give it to the applicant in a sealed envelope to hand in.
