  • Apply – licence details
  • Check your answers
  • Payment page (Card + BimPay)

  • Payment success

MDA admin pages

  • Sign in
  • Dashboard
  • Payments list
  • Payment detail
  • TSA reconciliation
  • Refunds & chargebacks
  • Users, roles & audit
  • Services & fees
  • Reports & exports

Treasury pages

  • Treasury dashboard
  • Ministries & departments
  • Cross-MDA TSA reconciliation
  • Approvals queue
  • Government-wide audit log

Prototype – not a working service

All data is mocked. No payments are taken. Forms do not submit anywhere. Built on the alpha.gov.bb design system.
