Pay for a government service

Pay for government services online. You can pay by debit or credit card, or with BimPay.

Most used

Renew your driver's licence

Renew online for 1, 3 or 5 years. From BDS$80.

Apply for a police certificate of character

Pay the BDS$50 application fee and book a fingerprinting appointment.

Pay your land tax

Pay your annual property tax bill online. Discounts available for early payment.

Pay a CAIPO annual return fee

File and pay your company's annual return at the Corporate Affairs and Intellectual Property Office.
