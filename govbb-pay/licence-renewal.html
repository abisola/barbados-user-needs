Barbados Licensing Authority

Renew your driver's licence

You can renew your Barbadian driver's licence online if it expires within the next 90 days. You can choose to renew for 1, 3 or 5 years.

Start now

Before you start

You'll need:

  • your National Registration Number (NRN)
  • your current driver's licence number
  • a debit or credit card, or a BimPay wallet

How long it takes

The online application takes about 5 minutes.

You will get an email with your new licence within 5 working days.

Fees

Renewal period Fee
1 yearBDS$80.00
3 yearsBDS$200.00
5 yearsBDS$320.00

There is no surcharge for paying online.

If you cannot renew online

Visit any Licensing Authority branch with your current licence and a valid ID.
